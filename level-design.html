Stockyard and Stables

Roles: Level design, block out, prop placement, QA/testing (light complexity, navigability, accessibility)

Tools: UE5, landscape tools, foliage tools

Resources: YouTube video and article by Amin Montazeri about level design process and principles.

This area is ta central hub where players can buy and upgrade horses, as well as gather essential supplies. The exterior is a dusty, fenced yard with wagons and a large wooden barn. Inside, players can find stalls with horses, NPC caretakers, and a supply shop filled with saddles, gear, and equipment. It serves as both a practical stop for preparing and a lively social point in the city.

Farm

Roles: Level design, prop placement, QA/testing (light complexity, navigability, accessibility)

Tools: UE5, splines, landscape tools, foliage tools

A quieter point of interest designed as a low-intensity exploration zone. Wheat fields, fenced crops, and a weathered barn set the stage, with only a handful of hostile encounters. Instead of combat, the focus is on searching for resources. Players can find chests the in barns and fields containing currency, weapons, and ammo. The farm feels peaceful yet slightly tense, rewarding interactivity and curiosity.

Daredevil

Fan Game

Home Level (work in progress)

Roles: Level design, plan and layout, block out, environment art

Tools: UE5, Blender, Fab, splines, landscape tools, modeling tools

This serves as Daredevil’s base of operations, designed as an interactive hub with distinct zones that each serve a purpose. The kitchen allows the player to heal, while the living room offers a space to relax between missions. Progress can be saved in the upstairs bedroom, training is possible in the dojo, and the armory provides access to weapon selection. A gritty twist reimagines the bathroom as an interrogation room, where players can select missions.

Copper and Tin Mine (work in progress)

Roles: Level design, plan and layout, block out, prop placement, QA/testing for light complexity

Tools: UE5, splines, landscape tools, foliage tools, modeling tools, QA/Testing (light complexity, navigability, accessibility)

Resources: YouTube video by Ryan Laley, YouTube video by Yepkoo

A sprawling, maze-like network of tunnels, with walls supported by timbers and rails winding deep underground. Combat is minimal, but the real challenge lies in navigating the twisting passages to reach a high-value treasure hidden at the heart of the mine. Above ground, players find a fantasy-inspired settlement with a shimmering lake, rustic worker houses, and industrial buildings tied to the mining. The blend of natural beauty and gritty industry makes the mine both dangerous and rewarding, encouraging players to explore every dark corner.

Daredevil

Fan Game

Level One: Shipyard (work in progress)

Roles: Level design, plan and layout, block out, environment art

Tools: UE5, Blender, Fab, splines, landscape tools, modeling tools

Set in New York’s docks, this is a hostile combat environment filled with enemies and narrative clues. The docked ship contains a challenging boss fight. Smaller warehouses hide civilians in need of rescue, as well as valuable hints tied to the larger story. A heavily guarded security room brings waves of thugs, testing the player’s combat skills before advancing deeper into the operation.

Elixir Run: Saya's Trials

Group Project

Magical Forest

Roles: Level design, environment art, prop placement, general QA/testing

Tools: UE5, Blender, Fab, blueprints, splines, landscape tools, modeling tools

A mystical forest where players play as a fairy on a quest to gather rare glowing ingredients. Red mushrooms, sun berries, moon berries, and blue flowers are scattered in specific, carefully designed locations. The environment shifts between serene lakes, cascading waterfalls, and shadowy forests, creating both beauty and mystery. Once the ingredients are collected, players must reach the portal to complete the run, crafting their finds into potions.
