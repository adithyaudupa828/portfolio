About Me

I’m a Game Designer with a focus on Level and Systems Design, passionate about creating gameplay that feels both engaging and meaningful. My portfolio includes group projects and personal work, where I’ve created levels from structured combat encounters to exploration-driven spaces. I also design game systems and mechanics that add to the game and enhance player experience.

I enjoy 3D modeling, which gives me a stronger appreciation for the art pipeline and how visuals support player experience. I’m always looking for ways to push mechanics, environments, and storytelling to deliver memorable gameplay moments.

I’m currently seeking opportunities in Game Design, where I can contribute my skills and passion to create compelling gaming experiences. If you have any questions or would like to connect, please don’t hesitate to reach out!
